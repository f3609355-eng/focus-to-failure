How fooocus works

      
    

    


      
      

        
The goal isn't longer sessions.
It's more total focus in your day.

        
Easy blocks. Generous breaks. Come back often. Volume compounds.

      


      
      

        
The loop

        

          

            
▶

            

              Focus
              Start a block. Goals are set below your floor — they should feel easy. Hide the timer with zen mode.
            

          

          
↓

          

            
✓

            

              Stop honestly
              Done = you chose to stop. Distracted = you'd already lost focus. The algorithm learns from the difference.
            

          

          
↓

          

            
☕

            

              Break, then come back
              Step away. The progress bar tracks your daily total. Each new block adds to it.
            

          

        

      


      
      

        
How the system adapts

        

          

            
📊 Daily volume goal

            
Your target total for the day — set just above what you reliably do (your volume floor). Hit it across as many sessions as you like.

          

          

            
🎯 Easy session goals

            
Each block targets ~90% of your session floor. Deliberately comfortable. You'll finish most of them — and start another.

          

          

            
⏱ Gap-based recovery

            
Back-to-back blocks get a small penalty. Wait 2 hours and you're fully fresh. Spreading sessions across morning, afternoon, and evening works best.

          

          

            
☕ Generous breaks

            
Breaks are longer early in the day to save your energy for later sessions. As you near your daily goal, they normalize.

          

        

      


      
      

        
Reading the numbers

        

          

            
Volume bar

            
Total focus today vs your daily goal. The main number. Fill it up.

          

          

            
Session floor

            
What you can reliably do in one sitting. Session goals are set below this.

          

          

            
Recovery %

            
How fresh you are since your last block. 100% = fully rested.

          

          

            
Milestones

            
Permanent trophies earned when your session floor crosses thresholds like 15m, 25m, 30m.

          

        

      


      
      

        
Tips

        

          

            Spread sessions out
            Morning + afternoon + evening beats three blocks in a row.
          

          

            Easy is the point
            Finishing a short block and starting another beats grinding one long one.
          

          

            Use zen mode
            The eye icon hides the timer. You'll focus better without watching it.
          

          

            Be honest
            Pressing Distracted isn't failure — it's data the algorithm needs.
          

          

            Take real breaks
            The generous early-day breaks are deliberate. Step away. Move. Come back.
          

          

            Watch the bar, not the clock
            Daily volume is the signal. Individual sessions are noise.
